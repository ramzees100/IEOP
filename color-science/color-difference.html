Color Difference

Introduction to perceptual color difference and Delta E.

Why Color Difference Is Needed

Two colors can have different numerical coordinates while appearing nearly identical, or similar coordinates while appearing visibly different.

Uniform Color Spaces

Spaces such as CIELAB were designed so that numerical distances more closely follow perceived differences.

Delta E

Delta E is a general name for formulas used to quantify the difference between two measured colors.
