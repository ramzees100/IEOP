About IEOP

Connecting light, information, and engineering.

The Institute of Electro-Optics & Photonics is an educational platform designed to make optics, photonics, color science, and communication systems easier to understand.

◎

Our Mission

IEOP aims to connect mathematical theory, physical understanding, simulation, experimentation, and engineering applications through clear educational resources.

✦

Our Vision

The long-term vision is to build an open learning platform for students, educators, researchers, engineers, and anyone interested in optical and electronic technologies.

Founder

Dr. Ibrahim Barka

Dr. Ibrahim Barka is an electrical engineer, educator, and researcher specializing in electro-optics, photonics, optical communications, fiber optics, communication systems, and related engineering fields.

IEOP was created to organize educational material and make advanced scientific concepts more accessible through explanations, figures, examples, mathematical models, MATLAB, and Python.
