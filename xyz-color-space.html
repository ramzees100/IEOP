CIE XYZ Color Space

Learn about CIE XYZ tristimulus values and color-matching functions.

Tristimulus Values

A color stimulus can be represented by three values: X, Y, and Z.

Role of Y

The Y tristimulus value is designed to correspond closely to photopic luminance.

Color-Matching Functions

The functions x̄(λ), ȳ(λ), and z̄(λ) describe the standard observer response used to calculate X, Y, and Z.
