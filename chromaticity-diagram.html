CIE 1931 Chromaticity Diagram

Introduction to CIE chromaticity coordinates and the CIE 1931 diagram.

Chromaticity Coordinates

Chromaticity coordinates are obtained by normalizing the tristimulus values: x = X/(X+Y+Z), y = Y/(X+Y+Z), and z = 1-x-y.

Spectral Locus

The curved boundary represents monochromatic visible wavelengths.

Color Mixtures

Straight lines between two chromaticity points represent additive mixtures of those two colors.
