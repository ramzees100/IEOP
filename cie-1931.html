CIE 1931 Standard Observer

Introduction to the CIE 1931 standard observer and colorimetric system.

Introduction

The CIE 1931 system standardized color matching for a representative two-degree visual field.

From RGB to XYZ

The experimental RGB data were transformed into the CIE XYZ system so that the color-matching functions could be represented without negative values.

Importance

CIE 1931 remains a foundation for color measurement, imaging, lighting, and display engineering.
