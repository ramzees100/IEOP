Color Science

Display Systems

Introduction to display primaries, gamut, calibration, and metrology.

Display Primaries

Displays reproduce colors by combining red, green, and blue primary emissions.

Color Gamut

The triangle formed by the display primaries on a chromaticity diagram represents the range of colors the display can reproduce.

Calibration and Metrology

Display calibration controls white point, luminance, tone response, and primary behavior using instruments such as colorimeters and spectroradiometers.
