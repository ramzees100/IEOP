Website Status

IEOP is under development. Color Science is currently available, while new tutorials, figures, simulations, and research resources will be added over time.

Founder

Dr. Ibrahim Barka

Dr. Ibrahim Barka is an electrical engineer, educator, and researcher specializing in electro-optics, photonics, optical communications, fiber optics, color science, and engineering education.

He founded IEOP to provide clear, structured, and accessible educational resources that connect scientific theory with simulation, experimentation, and practical engineering.
